Welcome to Discqualified

Where Every Throw Counts (even the bad ones)

New to disc golf? You’re in the right place. Discqualified is your go-to guide for learning the ropes, laughing at the flubs, and falling in love with the game—one throw at a time.

What We're About

At Discqualified, we believe that every throw counts—especially the bad ones. Our mission is to create a welcoming community for disc golf enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, you'll find valuable resources, tips, and a supportive network here.

Why "Discqualified"?

The name "Discqualified" is a playful take on the term "disqualified," reflecting our belief that every throw—good or bad—contributes to the learning process. We embrace mistakes and encourage players to laugh at their flubs, fostering a positive and inclusive environment.

Because we’ve all hit a tree five feet in front of us. We believe the best way to get better is to have fun, laugh at the bad shots, and keep throwing.

Join us on this journey as we explore the world of disc golf, share tips and tricks, and celebrate the joy of the game. Whether you're looking for advice on technique, gear recommendations, or just a good laugh, Discqualified has got you covered.

Ready to Play?

Great! Here are a few tips to help you get started:

  • Familiarize yourself with the basic rules of disc golf.
  • Practice your throwing technique in an open area.
  • Join a local disc golf club or community to meet other players.
  • Have fun and don't be afraid to make mistakes!
