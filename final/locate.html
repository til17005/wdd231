Locations

Spanish Fork, Utah

Depending on where you live you may find yourself surrounded by disc golf courses, and just a short drive away! unfortunately, many of you will find that you have to drive a bit to find a course. For this I reccommend and app called PDGA Course Directory. This app will help you find courses near you, and even help you find courses in other areas. It is a great resource for finding courses, and it is free to use. You can also find a list of courses in your area by searching online or checking with local disc golf clubs.

There is also an app you can use online or on your phone called UDisc. This app provides a comprehensive directory of disc golf courses, along with user reviews and ratings. It's a great tool for finding new courses to play and keeping track of your rounds. UDisc can be found here.

I highly recommend trying out both of these apps to enhance your disc golf experience!

I live in Utah, and I have found both of these apps to be incredibly helpful in finding new courses to play.

Locally in the Spanish Fork, Utah area I have many options within just a few miles. Some of my favorite courses include:

  • Urban Forest / Spanish Fork
  • Eagle Rock / Maple Mountain
  • Utah State Hospital / Provo
  • Jolly's Ranch / Springville
