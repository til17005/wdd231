Final Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Discqualified

The domain discqualified.com is available, but I will not be making that puchase.

Site Purpose:

This project is a disc golf website that will include information about discs, locations, and a newsletter subscription. This is an activity that my son-in-laws, one of my daughters, and I have recently become engaged in. We have found great joy in getting outside and even competing in friendly rounds. I thought creating a web page based on one of my hobbies would be a fun idea, and could be helpful for others looking to start.

Scenarios:

The target audience is disc golf enthusiasts, ranging from beginners to advanced players.

What do the numbers on the disc mean?

What are hyzer and anhyzer?

Where can I find a disc golf course near me?

How can I subscribe to the newsletter for updates and news?

Content Structure:

  • Home Page: Introduction and overview of the site.
  • Discs: Information about different types of discs.
  • Locations: Information and small list of disc golf courses.
  • Newsletter: Subscription form for updates and news.
